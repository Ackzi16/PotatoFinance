Permission preview

Example copy for consent and trust.

What we read: App name, timestamp, amount, merchant/payee text.
What we do not read: Chats, social notifications, personal messages.
  • Process only selected finance apps.
  • Classify notifications and ignore non-finance events.
  • Allow manual fallback if permission is denied.
